Georgia Study

Benchmarking Deeplasia on a Georgian Cohort

External benchmarking of medical AI systems across different ethnicities is crucial for ensuring the fairness and efficacy of these technologies. In this project, and in collaboration with experts in Georgia, we are working on evaluating the performance of Deeplasia on a pediatric cohort from Georgia.

Stay tuned!
